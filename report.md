# Sanctions Checking: Value Proposition & Market Analysis

## 1. Current Pain Points & Process (Greece Benchmark)
Currently, generating a single sanctions report is highly labor-intensive and costly.

- **Time Required:** 12 hours per report (with at least half of that time directly manageable/optimizable).
- **Current Hourly Rate:** €250 / hour.
- **Cost per Report:** €3,000.
- **Current Volume:** 30 to 40 deals per month.

### Monthly Revenue (Current - Max Volume)
- **Base Revenue:** 40 deals × €3,000 = **€120,000 / month**
- **Revenue with VAT (24%):** **€148,800 / month** (Greece)

---

## 2. Projected Value & Scalability
By streamlining the sanctions checking process, capacity can be significantly increased without adding proportional overhead.

### Projected Monthly Revenue (Optimized)
Assuming the workflow optimizations allow deal capacity to double (**×2**):
- **Projected Volume:** 60 to 80 deals per month.
- **Base Revenue (x):** 80 deals × €3,000 = **€240,000 / month**
- **Revenue with VAT 24% (y):** **€297,600 / month** (Greece)
- **Revenue Growth (X):** This represents a **100% (2×) increase** in revenue, adding an absolute **€148,800** in total gross monthly volume.

---

## 3. Global & US Valuation Equivalents
While the baseline uses Greek pricing models, the pain point is significantly more expensive in the United States, indicating an even higher value proposition.

*   **US Maritime Lawyer Rates:** Prominent US maritime law firms frequently bill between **$500 and $1,000+ per hour** for specialized corporate and sanctions compliance work.
*   **Equivalent US Report Cost:** Assuming a midpoint rate of **$750/hour**, a comparable 12-hour sanctions report in the US costs **$9,000** (compared to ~$3,200 / €3,000 in Greece).
*   **Implication:** A system saving time on these checks carries a 3× higher financial impact for US-based or international firms operating in premium markets.

### Monthly Revenue Equivalents (US Market)
Applying the $9,000 average US report cost to the volume models:
- **US Baseline Revenue (Current - 40 deals):** 40 deals × $9,000 = **$360,000 / month**
- **US Projected Revenue (Optimized - 80 deals):** 80 deals × $9,000 = **$720,000 / month**

> [!TIP]
> **Tax Treatment in the US**  
> Unlike Greece's flat 24% VAT, the US has no federal VAT, and professional legal services are exempt from state sales taxes in the vast majority of US jurisdictions. Therefore, for US firms, the gross revenue amounts above map exactly to the net top-line revenue without tax inflation. This means a fully optimized US team could be realizing roughly **$8.6 million annually** ($720k × 12) from this single compliance service.
